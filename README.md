## üî¶üí°Key Findings & Strategic Recommendations
The analysis identified critical areas for business intervention and resulted in the following actionable insights:

| Finding Category | Key Insights | Business Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Profit Leakage** | High discounts (over 40%) in specific sub-categories lead to significant losses, with high-risk orders exceeding **-$3,700**. | Requires immediate policy adjustment on discount caps for identified sub-categories. |
| **Category Strategy** | **Technology** drives the majority of high-margin profit. **Furniture** contains all loss-making sub-categories (Tables, Bookcases). | Focus promotional resources on Technology; initiate review of pricing and cost for Furniture loss leaders. |
| **Market Resilience** | The dataset validates strong market resilience, identifying a **-2.83% sales dip in 2015** followed by a strong **29% recovery in 2016**. | Confirms the effectiveness of strategic pivots and the need for continuous YoY performance monitoring. |
| **Service Risk** | The **East region** consistently shows the highest return count, despite not being the highest sales region. | Suggests potential issues with regional logistics, shipping quality, or product handling that require investigation. |
